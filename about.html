About me

Who I am

I'm Riccardo Battiato, I'm 21 and I study Computer Engineering in Italy.
I have nothing but two rules with myself: never stop learning, always be flexible.

If I had to be short I would define myself as a CSS developer right now: my portfolio consists of quickly created HTML & CSS websites, and you could clearly see my growth as a CSS Dev from the first project to the latest.
However, this wouldn't be entirely true; in fact I'm not just a CSS Developer.
I've been creating and sending online Wordpress websites since I was 15 years old, learnt basics of SEO, photo and video editing — there is a lot more.

I like to explore, to experiment and to challenge myself; in all of my projects you can clearly recognize sometimes the mistakes of a newbie and other times ingenious solutions — most of the times, both of these come from my curiosity, will to challenge myself and learn.
In fact, there is one greatest feature about me that everything I do has in common — and this isn't a company slogan or a marketing initiative — I am (truly) fueled by passion.
